ZERO BASE

ZERO BASE, the beginning of a new collection that selects works based on one’s own judgment rather than someone else’s standards.
It is an auction that provides a window for artists who have been working steadily to showcase their works, and offers collectors a new option to choose from.
Min Hee Kim
1988

The artist conveys a message through the appearance of the extinct dodo. We offer an experienced encouragement to the drifting young people not to lose their ideals.

The dodo bird in the work is not a “flyless foolish bird” but a creature with the possibility of flying again.
