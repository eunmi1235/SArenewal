VIP Customer Marketing with Arts and Culture

Seoul Auction, which is a strong proponent of transparency in the art market, has built a brand that is widely trusted both in Korea and overseas. On that basis, Seoul Auction undertakes professional arts/culture marketing for companies in diverse ways. It forges relationships with a wide range of brands, from luxury to financial corporations, and provides extraordinary services for the VIP customers of these companies. Offer your VIP customers an elegant and novel experience in the art-filled spaces of Seoul Auction.

Details and Expected benefits
  • Develop company’s long-term strategies and strengthen corporate competitiveness through arts/culture marketing
  • Provide the bespoke art contents/services that enhance each company’s objectives to differentiate in branding
  • Offer exclusive art experiences and expand channel of communication with the company’s existing customers through arts and culture
  • Improve the brand image which is more elegant and stylish through the partnership with Seoul Auction

Special Auction

Based on its extensive expertise and experience, Seoul Auction has designed a variety of fascinating auctions over the years in collaboration with luxury brands. The special auction features a wide range of items (car, wine, jewelry, designer furniture, etc.) and promotes the brand’s exclusive items effectively. By planning a special auction, expect positive synergy by introducing your products to Seoul Auction customers, who have an excellent eye for art and acquire high-priced artworks.

Details and Expected benefits
  • Provision of infrastructure (personnel, facilities, etc.) required for an exhibition and/or auction
  • Product exposure through exhibition(s) at Seoul Auction and greater recognition of the company in its field
  • PR/marketing through advertisements and/or product pages in Seoul Auction’s catalogue
  • Generation of optimum auction outcomes and PR effect through consultations with a Seoul Auction expert
  • Networking with collectors and art enthusiasts who have an ongoing relationship with Seoul Auction
  • Promotion of a brand and/or relevant auctions through Seoul Auction’s promotion channels

Corporate Art Service

Seoul Auction specializes in helping unerring and systematic management of artworks. From the purchase to the maintenance of artworks, our specialists provide professional consultation and discover the inherent value of your collection.

Valuation

From ownership of a single work of art to a large collection, come to Seoul Auction for an honest appraisal of Korean and international artworks.

Purchasing

Consult with a Seoul Auction expert on the entire process of purchasing an artwork, from budget allocation to artwork analysis, determining an appropriate purchase time, and insurance & shipping.

Selling

Seoul Auction helps corporations find the ideal way to sell artworks. Based on comprehensive consideration of the ever-changing conditions of the art market, we assist with determining the prices of artworks and deciding whether to sell via auction, private sale, or some other platforms.

Care Service

Seoul Auction conveniently and safely undertakes every step of the corporate art acquisition process, from proposing/curating artworks that best complement the given corporate space to shipping, installation, inspection of the artwork’s condition, and removal. Comprehensive care service is offered in conjunction with consulting on the management, maintenance, and improvement of the company’s existing art collection.
