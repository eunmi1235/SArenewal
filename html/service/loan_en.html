Art Collateral Loans

Seoul Auction offers loans to art owners that are based on artworks and/or antiques as collateral.

Emails on art financing sent to Seoul Auction should not include the word “collateral” or “loan” in the title, as this usually causes the email to be automatically classified as spam (and possibly never being read).
STEP 01 Application via email

Photo and information on the artwork is sent by email to Seoul Auction.

STEP 02 Sending of reply on loan viability

After an assessment of whether a collateral loan can be provided for the artwork, an email is sent by the employee in charge.

STEP 03 Valuation of artwork

After the artwork (physical version) is received, an assessment of its authenticity and price is conducted.

STEP 04 Sending of reply on whether loan will be provided

An email is sent on whether the sender has been approved for the desired loan.

STEP 05 In-person submission of documents

An in-person visit is made to a Seoul Auction location to submit the required documents (based on customer type: individual or corporate).

STEP 06 Sending of funds (with guidelines / explanatory letter)

After the document screening is completed, the funds are wired within one week (accompanied by explanatory letter / list of guidelines regarding loan).

Required documents: individual customer
  • Personal seal
  • 1 copy of personal seal validity certificate (issued within past three months)
  • 1 copy of legal ID
  • 1 copy of bankbook
  • 1 Certificate of Earned Income (National Tax Service)
  • 1 Payment Verification form (National Tax Service)
  • 1 Proof of Local Tax Payment (Community Service Center)

If you have overdue taxes, your application will not be accepted.

Your loan will not be authorized unless all of the above documents have been presented. Please make sure to submit all required documents.

Types of collateral accepted

Artworks that can be sold through an auction

E.g. works of modern/contemporary art, works of ancient art (excluding Chinese ceramics)

Required documents: corporate customer
  • Copy of business license
  • 1 copy of corporate seal validity certificate (issued within past three months)
  • Corporate seal, CEO’s seal, and certified copy of corporate register (issued within past three months)
  • Validity certificate for CEO’s seal (issued within past three months)
  • Copy of legal ID (of CEO or representative)
  • Copy of board of directors’ meeting minutes
  • Copy of bankbook
  • Previous year’s audit report
  • Previous year’s Certificate of VAT Tax Base
  • Tax Assessment Certificate and Certificate of Tax Payment

If your application is submitted by proxy, 1. the proxy’s ID and a 2. sealed power of attorney are also required.

Your loan will not be authorized unless all of the above documents have been presented. Please make sure to submit all required documents.

Conditions for artwork-backed loans
Type Collateral loan
Interest rate 12% per year
Overdue interest rate 15% per year
Loan period (basic) 3 months
Interest Principle: payment in advance
Credit line 50% of appraisal amount

Conditions above are adjustable depending on each case.
