Insa Art Storage

Store artworks and/or other valuables in the heart of Seoul in a specialized storage facility operated by Seoul Auction. Insa Art Storage is located in Insa-dong, a district full of galleries and art supply stores that is easily reached via public transportation.

Insa Art Storage has units of varying sizes (between 2.8 and 8.3 square meters, accessible separately with individual keys), allowing the storage of a wide range of objects. There is also a private cabinet section where valuables can be kept safely while remaining conveniently accessible.

Art Storage System
HVAC system

24-hour maintenance of optimum temperature and humidity levels per artwork

  • Temperature: 18 to 22 degrees Celsius / Humidity: 45 to 55%
  • Maintained year-round (figures can be adjusted if necessary)
Emergency ventilation system

Extinguishing of fires (with halon gas) without damaging artworks

  • Centralized fire alarm system
  • Automatically-activated heat/smoke detectors
Security system

Cutting-edge security system operated on 24-hour basis in partnership with Secom, Korea’s top security service provider

  • CCTV surveillance (recorded on 24-hour basis)
  • Strict control/management of all who enter or exit

Jangheung Art Storage

Jangheung Art Storage offers units of varying sizes (34 to 67 square meters) and specializes in the safe storage of large artworks and bulk items.
